WHAT'S NEW - 7-DAY RECIPE SYSTEM
================================================================================

The app now correctly supports WKLY Nuts' unique product structure:

BEFORE:
- Each SKU had ONE recipe for all sachets
- Weekly Pack = 7 identical sachets ‚ùå

NOW:
- Each SKU has 7 DIFFERENT recipes (MON through SUN) ‚úÖ
- Weekly Pack = 7 unique sachets (1 per day) ‚úÖ
- Monthly Pack = 28 sachets (4 of each day) ‚úÖ

EXAMPLE:
Night Pack has different ingredients/quantities for each day:
- Monday: 13 ingredients, 44g total (unique mix)
- Tuesday: 13 ingredients, 44g total (different proportions)
- Wednesday: 13 ingredients, 44g total (different proportions)
... and so on for all 7 days!

Production Calculation Example:
Produce 80 Monthly Packs:
- Monthly Pack = 4 Weekly Packs
- Need: 80 √ó 4 = 320 of EACH day's sachet
- If Monday has 6g Almonds: 320 √ó 6g = 1,920g needed
- Calculate for all 7 days, then sum by ingredient

For full details, see UPDATES.md file!
