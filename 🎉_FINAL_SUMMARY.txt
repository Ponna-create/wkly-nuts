HOW TO USE THE APP:
================================================================================

STEP 1: START THE APP
---------------------
cd "D:\POKAsoft\wkly nuts app\wkly nuts app\vendor and product management tool"
npm install
npm run dev

Open: http://localhost:5173


STEP 2: CREATE VENDOR WITH INGREDIENTS
---------------------------------------
1. Click "Vendor Management"
2. Click "Create Vendor"
3. Fill in vendor details
4. Add ingredients (one by one):
   - Name: e.g., "Almonds"
   - Quantity: e.g., 100
   - Unit: kg
   - Price: e.g., 500 (‚Çπ500/kg)
   - Quality: 5 stars
5. Click "Save Vendor"

‚úÖ Data saved automatically!


STEP 3: CREATE SKU WITH 7-DAY RECIPES
--------------------------------------
1. Click "SKU Management"
2. Click "Create SKU"
3. Fill basic info:
   - Name: "Night Pack"
   - Description: "Evening blend"
   - Target Weight: 30 grams per sachet
4. Click "Next: Build Day Recipes"

5. For MONDAY (blue tab):
   - Add 6 ingredients @ 5g each = 30g total
   - Watch weight indicator turn green!

6. Click "Next Day" or click TUE tab
   - Build TUESDAY recipe (can be different!)

7. Repeat for all 7 days (WED, THU, FRI, SAT, SUN)
   - Each day gets unique recipe
   - Progress shows "7/7 Complete"

8. Click "Save SKU"

‚úÖ Data saved automatically!


STEP 4: CALCULATE PRODUCTION
-----------------------------
1. Click "Production Calculator"
2. Select: "Night Pack"
3. Select: "Monthly Pack (28 sachets)"
4. Enter: 80 packs
5. Click "Calculate Requirements"

See results:
- Consolidated ingredients (total across 7 days)
- Day-by-day breakdown
- Vendor recommendations
- Shortage alerts
- Total costs

6. Click "Export CSV" or "Print"


STEP 5: SET PRICING
-------------------
1. Click "Pricing Strategy"
2. Select SKU and pack type
3. Enter all costs
4. Set profit margin (slider)
5. See suggested selling price
6. Click "Save Pricing Strategy"

‚úÖ Data saved automatically!


STEP 6: PLAN SALES TARGETS
---------------------------
1. Click "Sales & Revenue"
2. Add target rows
3. View projections
4. See break-even analysis
5. Review raw material needs

‚úÖ Data saved automatically!


STEP 7: EXPORT & BACKUP
-----------------------
1. Go to Dashboard
2. Find "Data Management" card

To share with team:
- Click "Export Excel"
- Email the .xlsx file

To backup:
- Click "Backup (JSON)"
- Save file to safe location

Every Friday: Do both! ‚úÖ
